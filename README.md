 Executive Summary
Ecometricus is a hospitality-focused intelligence platform that helps hotel F&B teams increase profit margins while reducing food waste, water, and energy consumption.
Unlike hardware-heavy competitors, we deliver a Chef-friendly, software-only solution that merges operational KPIs with sustainability metrics charts in real time.
Why Now?
Hotels face rising food costs, volatile utilities and a new generation of guests demanding fast-approaching and commitment on climate reporting. Food waste, water, and energy inefficiency are no longer environmental concerns — they are direct profit leaks. Acting now isn’t a trend; it is a strategic and operational imperative to remain competitive, compliant, and financially resilient.
